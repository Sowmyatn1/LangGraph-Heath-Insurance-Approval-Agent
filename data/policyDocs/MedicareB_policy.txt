MedicareB Plan Policy Document

Office Visits (CPT 99213):
- Covered under all circumstances except non-covered services
- No prior authorization

MRI Brain (CPT 70551):
- Covered only for patients with documented seizures (ICD10 G40.x) or brain tumor
- Prior authorization required
- Max one MRI Brain per year

Ingrown Toenail Surgery (CPT 11721):
- Covered, but requires prior authorization
- Only if outpatient; inpatient cases reviewed case by case
