MedicareA Plan Policy Document

Coverage for Physical Therapy (HCPCS G0296):
- Beneficiaries aged 65 or older
- Must submit prior authorization before treatment begins
- Max visits per year: 20
- Exclusions: therapy for non-medically necessary cosmetic or wellness purposes

MRI Lumbar Spine (CPT 72148):
- Covered if there is a diagnosis of a herniated disk or severe spinal stenosis
- Prior authorization required
- One MRI per year unless significant change in symptoms

Electrocardiogram (EKG, CPT 93000):
- Covered as routine diagnostic procedure
- No prior authorization required
- No visit limit per year
